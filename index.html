Donation Requirements

Age & Weight

  • ✓ Must be 18-65 years old
  • ✓ Minimum weight of 110 lbs (50 kg)
  • ✓ Must be in good health

Before Donation

  • ✓ Get adequate sleep (8 hours)
  • ✓ Eat a healthy meal
  • ✓ Stay hydrated

After Donation

  • ✓ Rest for at least 5-10 minutes
  • ✓ Consume fluids and snacks
  • ✓ Avoid strenuous activities for 24 hours

Frequently Asked Questions

The actual blood donation takes about 8-10 minutes, but the entire process including registration, screening, and refreshment takes about 30-45 minutes.

You can donate whole blood every 56 days (8 weeks). However, you should wait at least 48 hours between different types of donations.

Yes, blood donation is very safe. All equipment used is sterile and disposable. Your body replaces the donated blood within 24-48 hours.

Most donors feel fine after donation. Some may experience slight dizziness, but it passes quickly. Eating, drinking, and resting helps.

There are 8 main blood types: O+, O-, A+, A-, B+, B-, AB+, and AB-. O- is the universal donor, while AB+ is the universal recipient.

While not mandatory, registering helps us prepare and speeds up the donation process. You can register online or at the camp.
